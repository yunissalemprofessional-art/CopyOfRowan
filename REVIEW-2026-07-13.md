# Dashboard Review — July 13, 2026

Reviewed the live site (copy-of-rowan.vercel.app) + full code. No console errors on the live site. Below: what I fixed, what I found but didn't touch, and how your 9 domains map.

## Bugs fixed (already edited in your files)

1. **Goal streak could never go above 0** (`index.html`)
   `rollover()` deleted every past day's goals before `processStreak()` could count them, so no day ever registered as "completed." Reordered so the streak is processed first. Your ⚡ streak will actually work now.

2. **Day ring used the wrong hours** (`index.html`)
   Ring percent was computed on 5:00 AM–10:00 PM while the label under it (and your actual targets) say 6:00 AM–10:30 PM. The "% of day" and "awake time left" numbers were always slightly off. Aligned to 6:00 AM–10:30 PM.

3. **Goal streak didn't sync across devices** (`index.html`)
   `goal_streak_v1` was missing from the cloud-sync key list. Added.

4. **Gym split didn't match your real week** (`gym.html`)
   It was a rolling 3-day Push→Pull→Legs cycle that advanced every calendar day — tomorrow (Tue) would've said "Pull" when your Tuesday is Sprints, and it would drift forever. Migrated to your fixed weekly split: **Mon Push / Tue Sprints / Wed Pull / Thu Rest / Fri Legs / Sat Sprints / Sun Rest.** Sprint days show your protocol (jog warm-up, 6–8 × 100m, cooldown); Rest days say rest. The migration runs once automatically on next load, on every device.

5. **Exercises were the template's defaults, not your routine** (`gym.html`)
   The list was Hevy's beginner program (Pec Deck, Shrugs, Leg Extension...). Replaced with your actual split: Push = Bench, OHP, Incline DB Press, Tricep Pushdown, Lateral Raises. Pull = Deadlift, Lat Pulldown, Barbell Row, Face Pulls, Bicep Curls. Legs = Squat, RDL, Leg Press, Leg Curl, Calf Raises.
   ⚠️ Any sets you logged on removed exercises (e.g. Pec Deck) are still in storage but no longer visible. Kept exercises (Bench, Lat Pulldown, Face Pull, Lateral Raise, Tricep Pushdown, Leg Press, Leg Curl, Calf Raise) keep their history.

6. **Timezone bug shifted the rotation by a day** (`gym.html`)
   The anchor date was parsed as UTC midnight, which in US timezones is the *previous* day — silently moving your whole split one day off. Now parsed as local time.

7. **Sprint/Rest days would've shown Push exercises** (`gym.html`)
   A bad fallback defaulted any unknown day to the first lifting day. Fixed.

8. **Weekly content counts flipped a day early after midnight** (`content.html`)
   Everything else uses your 6 AM day boundary, but the weekly per-platform counts and the Monday stats reset used raw calendar dates. Aligned.
